## What is NoonFlow?

NoonFlow is a visual AI coding workspace for people who already use Claude Code and Codex heavily.

## Is this a replacement for Claude Code or Codex?

No. NoonFlow sits on top of those runtimes and gives you a stronger visual workspace around them.

## Is this just a wrapper?

It is built on top of the underlying CLIs, but the goal is not just to launch commands. The point is better workflow management: sessions, memory, skills, providers, costs, and project visibility.

## Do I still need Claude Code or Codex CLI installed?

Yes. The current product depends on the underlying runtimes being installed and authenticated.

## Can I use custom providers or relay-compatible APIs?

Yes. NoonFlow is designed to support flexible provider and model setup, including custom endpoints and relay-compatible APIs.

## Is macOS the only platform right now?

For the current public release, yes. Broader platform support is planned after the macOS release is more stable.

## What feedback is most helpful?

Concrete feedback is the most useful:

- what you were trying to do
- what worked well
- what felt confusing
- what broke
- what you wish existed next
